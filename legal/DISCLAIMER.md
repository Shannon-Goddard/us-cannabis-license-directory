**Specific limitations include:**

- **California confidential records** (~6,400 entries) have no street address, city, or ZIP code. The California Department of Cannabis Control does not publish this information.
- **Geocoding coverage is approximately 49.5%.** Records without street addresses cannot be geocoded.
- **Fuzzy matching flags** (`sells_seeds`, `license_usda`) are algorithmic approximations, not confirmed facts. False positives are expected.
